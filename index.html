Getting Started with SolarManager

Welcome to SolarManager, your comprehensive solar energy management system. This guide will help you get started with monitoring and optimizing your solar power system.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your system's performance, including:

  • Real-time energy production metrics
  • Battery status and health information
  • Energy consumption patterns
  • System alerts and notifications
  • Forecasts and planning tools

Navigation

Use the sidebar to navigate between different sections of the system:

  • Dashboard: Main overview of system performance
  • Generation: Detailed solar production data
  • Battery: Battery status and management tools
  • Consumption: Energy usage statistics and patterns
  • Analytics: Historical data and performance trends
  • Settings: System configuration options

Getting Assistance

If you need further assistance, please refer to the other help sections or contact our support team at support@solarmanager.com or call +1 (800) 123-4567 between 9 AM and 5 PM EST.
